Privacy Policy

>_Nexus — SSH Terminal Shell

Nexus does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Storage

Nexus stores the following data locally on your device:

  • SSH connection profiles (host, port, username, display name) — stored as JSON in the app's sandboxed Application Support directory.
  • SSH passwords and private keys — stored in the iOS Keychain, protected by your device passcode and Secure Enclave.
  • Host key fingerprints — stored locally to verify server identity on subsequent connections.
  • Theme and font preferences — stored in UserDefaults within the app sandbox.

None of this data leaves your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Network Connections

Nexus connects only to SSH servers that you explicitly configure. These connections are made directly from your device to the server you specify — there is no intermediary, proxy, or relay. We have no visibility into your connections or terminal sessions.

Analytics and Tracking

Nexus does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks. We do not track app usage in any way.

Third-Party Services

Nexus does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Face ID

If your device supports Face ID, iOS may use it to authenticate access to credentials stored in the Keychain. Face ID data is managed entirely by iOS and is never accessible to Nexus.

Children's Privacy

Nexus is a general-purpose utility and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Material changes will be noted with an updated effective date.

Contact
