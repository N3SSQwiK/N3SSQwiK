Privacy Policy

>_Nexus — SSH Terminal Shell · Developed by N3SSQwiK

>_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Storage

>_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell stores the following data locally on your device:

  • SSH connection profiles (host, port, username, display name, and connection metadata) — stored as JSON in the app's sandboxed Application Support directory.
  • SSH passwords and private keys — stored in the iOS Keychain, protected by your device passcode and iOS Data Protection.
  • Host key fingerprints — stored locally to verify server identity on subsequent connections.
  • Theme and font preferences — stored in UserDefaults within the app sandbox.

None of this data leaves your device or is transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Network Connections

>_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell connects only to SSH servers that you explicitly configure. These connections are made directly from your device to the server you specify — there is no intermediary, proxy, or relay. The developer has no visibility into your connections or terminal sessions.

Analytics and Tracking

>_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks. No app usage data is tracked or collected in any way.

Third-Party Libraries

>_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell uses open-source libraries (SwiftTerm, swift-nio-ssh, swift-crypto) for terminal emulation and SSH connectivity. None of these libraries collect or transmit user data.

Data Deletion

All data stored by >_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell resides entirely on your device. You can delete individual host profiles and their associated credentials from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all remaining data, including Keychain entries, saved profiles, host key fingerprints, and preferences.

Children's Privacy

>_Nexus - SSH Terminal Shell does not collect personal information from any user, including children under 13.

International Users

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted to the developer, there is no personal data processing as defined by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You are the sole controller of your data. We do not sell, share, or rent any personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Changes to This Policy
